Instruction for the presentation of manuscripts for publication in Paléorient. Manuscripts should be typewritten in double space with margins of at least 5 cm. Send twa copies. A ten line abstract in french and english should be enclosed on a separate sheet. Footnotes should be listed-at the end of the text. Within a composed figure, every drawing should be numbered: 1, 2, 3, 4 etc. . . Folding should be avoided. In any case never give photocopies for publication.
