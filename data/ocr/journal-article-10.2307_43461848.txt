<plain_text><page sequence="1">THREE POEMS 335 SONG FOR A HOLLOW BOWL Combined within the perfect hollow of a bowl All that men have spoken for with gods Of their own separate fashioning And beauty cut with wings, A magic insistency: The minutiae of detail to speak of rendering Composite verities into a visible paradigm And magnify the soul Of an imponderable universe. Birdnote to amplify a human mythology With marginal denotations of alien realms Translated for crystallized interpretations Of anthropomorphic rituals': And the sky a darkened bluebell By night, a sombre death.</page></plain_text>
