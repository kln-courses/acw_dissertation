The meeting will be held at the Portland Marriott, which is on the Willamette River Front in an area featuring parks, restaurants and shops. The Civic Auditorium and theaters are within one block, and Old Town is within walking distance. Portland has excellent public transportation. The Marriott has a health club with a small swimming pool and hot tub, so bring appropriate apparel. The Portland Marriott's mailing address is 1401 S.W. Front Avenue, Portland, Oregon 97201. Telephone is (503)226-7600. You are urged to make your reservation early because the hotel management will begin to release the APA's block of rooms one month before the meeting. A hotel reservation form can be found at the back of this issue. Travel Information Driving from the South on 1-5: Exit City Center (Exit 299B). Then take Front Street (Exit 1A). After two traffic lights, you will see the Marriott on your left. Driving from the North on I-5 or from the East on 1-84: Take the City Center and then the Front Street exits, as above. Take Front Street to the Marriott. By Air: There is excellent transportation on the Raztranz Airporter from Portland International Airport directly to the Marriott. The bus departs from the airport two times hourly between 5:10 a.m. and 11:00 p.m. The trip takes about half an hour, except at rush hour. Pre-Registration and Registration The registration fee is $5.00 for student members, $15.00 for regular members, and $20.00 for non-members of the APA. Only those registered may attend the Reception on Thursday evening, but tickets for guests may be purchased at the Convention Registration desk or at the door. A Pre-Registration form is included in the back of this issue of the Proceedings. People who are not now members of the APA may take advantage of the lower 7</page><page sequence="3">APA PROCEEDINGS, VOL. 65, NO. 4 registration fee by becoming members. Membership forms are available from the National Office and also at the Convention Registration desk. Presidential Address The Presidential Address will be delivered by Alvin Goldman at 9:00 p.m., Friday, March 27. Vice-President Patricia Churchland will introduce President Goldman. Receptions There will be a reception at 10:00 p.m. on Thursday, March 26, in Ballroom F, for registered participants. Tickets for guests may be purchased at the door or at the Convention Registration desk. The Presidential Reception will be held on Friday, March 25, in the ballroom lobby area. Business Meeting and Voting Membership The annual Business Meeting will be held at 6:00 p.m. on Friday, March 27, in Ballroom F. There will be reports from Divisional and National Officers and Committees. According to the Association's By-Laws, regular membership is given by an act of the Executive Director on behalf of the Board of Officers. Each year the list of regular members that appears in the September Proceedings is used as the list of eligible voters for the three subsequent Divisional business meetings.
