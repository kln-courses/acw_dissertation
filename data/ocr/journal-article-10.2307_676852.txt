Unsolicited manuscripts submitted by persons who are not members of the Associa- tion must be accompanied by a non-refundable processing fee of $25.00. The views expressed are those of the authors and not necessarily those of the Association or its officers. Persons wishing to obtain permission to republish material appearing in this journal may apply for forms and rates by writing to the Washington office. The AMERICAN ANTHROPOLOGIST is abstracted in Abstracts in Anthropology, Excerpta Medica, Historical Abstracts, America: His- tory and Life Human Resources Abstracts, Sage Urban Studies Abstracts and Sage Public Administration Abstracts; it is abstracted and indexed in United States Political Science Docu- ments; and is indexed in Social Sciences &amp; Humanities Index, and Anthropological Index to Current Periodicals (R.A.I.); book reviews are indexed in Book Review Index; the tables of contents for coming issues appear in Current Anthropology, ABC POL SCI (Advance Biblio- graphy of Contents: Political Science and Government), and Current Contents: Behavioral, Social, and Management Sciences.
