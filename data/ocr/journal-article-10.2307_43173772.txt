This outstanding large-format book describes the geographic location, keystone animals, ecology, and endangered species of eight broad habitat types. (Mira Cómo Crezco) Translated by Sara Sánchez. Barcelona: Edebé. 2004. 24 pp. each. $10.95 each. (Grades 1-2). This animal development series is an excellent in- troduction to the circle of life. Purists may disapprove of the anthropomorphism in the simple first-person text in which la mariposa (butterfly), el oso (bear), el pato (duckling), and la rana (frog) introduce them- selves and describe their habitat, growth, and life functions. Nonetheless, the clear color photographs and well-placed close-ups provide a most engaging view of the natural world for young Spanish speakers.
