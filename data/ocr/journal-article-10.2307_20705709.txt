Admission to membership of the Society is by application and is granted on the approval of the Council. Application forms are obtainable from the Secretary. Every person accepted for membership receives notice of his election from the Secretary, together with a copy of the rules, and on payment of the current year's subscription becomes entitled to all the benefits of membership, including free copies of the Journal. Thereafter, subscriptions are payable in advance, on January 1 each year. Annual membership fees are $(N.Z.)25.00 for New Zealand residents, $(N.Z.)28.00 for overseas members paying in New Zealand currency. Life membership, costing $(N.Z.)100.00, is restricted to those who have been members of the Society for 10 years or more and have reached 60 years of age.
