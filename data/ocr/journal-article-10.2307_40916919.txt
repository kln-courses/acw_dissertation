The bibliography is an annotated list of books, articles, and reviews dealing with the Scandinavian languages and literatures which appeared in the United States and Canada during 1967; it includes, moreover, scholarly works on Scandinavian subjects Americans have had published abroad, and American trans- lations from the Scandinavian. The bibliography includes primarily items of concern to those engaged in Scandinavian studies. The year is listed only when the item is a review of a book published before 1967 or when the item is one missed in assembling the bibliography for the previous year. A number within parentheses before an item refers to an entry for the same item in a previous bibliography. For SS, 39:4, 345-367 (for example), read Scandinavian Studies, Volume 39, Number 4, pages 345-367. For brief mentions of Scandinavian books, see Lawrence S. Thompson's column "Briefer Mention" in the Papers of the Bibliographical Society of America.
