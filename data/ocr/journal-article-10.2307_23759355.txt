<plain_text><page sequence="1">Information wanted / Fragen GUATEMALA (IDAEH). The Guatemalan Ministry of Culture and Sport announced on June 15,1988, the discovery of a mystifying sculpture in the form of an anthropomorphic head, 0.41 high x 0.34 cm wide, which was recently unear thed on Av. 1-16, zona 8, Panorama, Ciudad San Cristóbal. Since the piece was not found in association with any datable ceramics or other artifacts, the question of its cultural and chronological identity has yet to be solved. Although the Guatemalan Ministry has suggested an Olmec affinity for the sculpture, this remains in the realm of spéculation at this time.
