NOTICE TO AUTHORS All manuscript material must be typed double-spaced on one side of sheet only. Use white noncorrasable bond or copy paper. Submit original plus two copies. Include an abstract of less than 150 words that specifically refers to text material. Footnotes are not permitted. Text citations are set in parentheses, e.g., (Author 1995:359). References are listed alpha betically by author and in ascending chronological order. Number all figures consecu tively and provide a caption for each. Tables should be typed on separate pages, num bered suquentially, and given headings. Upon acceptance, all papers must be provided on a 3.5-inch diskette with word-processing program indicated, and all artwork must be cam era-ready: all illustrations must be black-on-white drawings or glossy prints.
