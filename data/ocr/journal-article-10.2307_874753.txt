Two are reviewed in the present issue: tapestries at the Mobilier National, and woodcuts at the Institut Nierlandais. Some of the others will form the subject of a leading article in the February issue entitled 'Le Sixieme Siecle Europeen' by Dr John Shearman. Unfortunately the most important of all, consisting of paintings and drawings from French public collections at the Petit Palais, will have closed by the time this article appears.
