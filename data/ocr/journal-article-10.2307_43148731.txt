(Grades 1-2) Here is that rarity, a commendable science trade book for the primary child. Written by an expert on animal behavior, this appealing "I Can Read" book tells how sea otters live, hunt, and care for their young in the cold Pacific Ocean. It reveals 48 October 1981 Science and Children</page><page sequence="2">some of the methods scientists use to observe otters within their natural environment.
