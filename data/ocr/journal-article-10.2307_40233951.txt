FRGS This issue of the Bibliography - like that in volume 56 no.l - includes items published mainly from 2001 to 2003. The form of entries reflects the order and punctuation conventions of ISBD (M): International Standard Bibliographic Description for Monographic Publications, Rev. ed. (London : International Federation of Library Associations and Institutions, 1987), but with modifications to accommodate articles in journals and col- lective works. Some English translations or paraphrases of titles have been supplied by the compiler. The abbreviation 'ill.' alone implies illustration (s) of a cartographic nature; where both 'ill.' and 'maps' occur together illustrations of a general and of a cartographic nature are included. The following symbols for lan- guage are used: Ar = Arabic, C = Chinese, D = German, E = English, F = French, Gr = Greek, He = Hebrew, Ja = Japanese, La = Latin, Nl = Dutch, Pi = Polish, R = Russian, Sp = Spanish and Sv = Swedish. These are taken from ISO R 639 : Symbols for languages, countries and authorities, Ed. 1 (International Standards Organisation, 1968). To facilitate indexing each entry is allocated a unique reference number, containing three elements: the last two digits of year of publication of the volume of Imago Mundi in which the 'Bibliography' appears, followed by the original series volume number, and finally a sequence number. Items with a * before the entry number have not been inspected by the Compiler.
