II. 73-150 Professor Driver's interesting resuscitation of suggestions for the emendation of the text of the Psalms4 errs on the side of generosity. Some, I think, had better remain in the obscurity from which he has rescued them. A few call for notice. (1) Ps. 74. 1. The proposal to tr. עשן 'heavy' or 'severe' in place of the traditional 'smoke', 'be wroth', has little to recommend it.
